Meet The Watson Group

Founded in 1970, The Watson Group is a respected manufacturer and distributor of quality, branded furniture across South Africa, Kenya, Ghana and Zambia. Renamed from Watson Brothers in 2023 after a new investor took a 45% stake, the business has just recorded its highest sales in company history alongside a second consecutive year of low profits after tax. Peter Watson has retired as Chairman and CEO. You are the new CEO.

Founded1970, as Watson Brothers
Footprint4 South African plants, 1 Ghana plant, ~2,500 employees
MarketsSouth Africa, Kenya, Ghana, Zambia
Ownership45% sold to a new investor in 2023
Gross salesOver R6.5bn, at a record high
The catchSecond consecutive year of low profit after tax

The old Watson model was conservative and paternalistic: fixed salaries with modest commissions, no compulsory retirement, and no dismissal without Peter Watson's personal review. Competitors have since out-marketed and out-returned Watson, product development has stalled, and digitalisation lags. This is the business you have just inherited.
